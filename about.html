    Our Foundation

    Mission, Vision & Story

    The principles that have guided every seed we've planted and every farmer we've served.

    01

    Our Story

    Founded in Bangalore in 2002, AgroFarm started as a two-person consultancy helping local Karnataka farmers access better seeds and soil data. Today we power smart agriculture across 18 Indian states.

    02

    Our Mission

    To democratize precision agriculture — making modern tools and knowledge accessible to every farmer, regardless of farm size or digital literacy, so they can grow more with less.

    03

    Our Vision

    A world where Indian agriculture leads globally — not just in volume, but in sustainability, quality, and farmer well-being. We see technology and nature as partners, not opposites.
